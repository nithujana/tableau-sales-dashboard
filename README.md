## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing sales performance across Europe.

## Key Insights

* Total Revenue: $1.70B
* Total Profit: $501.68M
* Units Sold: 6.58M
* Profit Margin: 29.45%

## Business Questions Answered

- What are the monthly revenue and profit trends throughout the year?
- Which product categories generate the highest sales revenue?
- How do online and offline sales channels compare in terms of revenue?
- What is the overall profit margin of the business?
- Which countries contribute the most to revenue and profit?
- Is there a relationship between revenue and profit across countries?
- Which products or categories should the company focus on to maximize profitability?
